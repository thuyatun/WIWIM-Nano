U Phyo Min Thein

U Phyo Min Thein (born 13 April 1969) is a Burmese politician, ex-political prisoner, currently serving as Chief Minister of Yangon Region and Yangon Region Parliament MP for Hlegu Township Constituency No. 2. He was a former chairman of Union Democratic Party and also a founder of People Democracy Party which was later merged with Union Democratic Party. He studied at Yangon University but did not graduate.

Dr. Aung Moe Nyo

Dr. Aung Moe Nyo, a doctor, is a Burmese politician, currently serving as Chief Minister of Magway Region, the head of the Magway Region Government. He was elected from NLD in the former elections and re-elected in the 2015 election and became the chief minister in 2016.
